Our Mission

Small Act, Big Impact.

We want to shine a light on MediaTek colleagues who dedicate their time to making the world better. It's not just a recognition — it's a tribute to the unseen kindness around us.

Whether you are caring for stray animals, storytelling at schools, or using your skills to help NGOs — if you are doing it regularly, you are the hero we are looking for.

Who is the Unseen Hero?

We're looking for someone who commits to volunteering regularly or long-term!

Your action doesn't need to be grand — it just needs to show your sustained commitment.

💡 1+ year earns bonus points  ·  Individual, team, or nominate someone — all welcome!
